Beyond games

Other Work

Alongside games, I have worked on Arduino cars and smaller electronics projects. These were hands-on builds where programming had to connect with wiring, motors, sensors, testing, and practical problem solving.

Arduino cars

Built several small vehicle prototypes, including my own car from scratch. It used two copper circuit boards as the base, an Arduino for control, its own battery, four wheel servos controlled through relays, a display for sensor and decision feedback, and ultrasonic sensors on both ends for navigation.

Electronics projects

Worked on smaller electrical builds that involved circuits, inputs, outputs, wiring, and making hardware behave reliably when tested in real conditions.

Practical prototyping

Learned to test ideas physically, find problems quickly, and improve projects through repeated small fixes.
